Stay Safe Online Checklist

Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or passwords with anyone
Always verify payment requests before approving them
Do not click on suspicious links sent via SMS or email
Enable two-factor authentication on all accounts
Use strong and unique passwords for different platforms
Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
Regularly check your bank statements for unusual activity
Install apps only from trusted sources like Play Store/App Store

 UPI / Financial Safety Tips:

Remember: you only need a UPI PIN to send money, not to receive it
Never accept unknown “collect request” payments without verification

National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
