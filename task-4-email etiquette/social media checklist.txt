# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

##  Do’s
- Think before you post and ensure your content is appropriate.
- Use privacy settings to control who can see your information.
- Share accurate and verified information only.
- Be respectful and polite in comments and messages.
- Maintain a professional profile (good photo, clear bio).

##  Don’ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly.
- Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.
- Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments.
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or unknown sources.
- Do not spread rumors or unverified news.
